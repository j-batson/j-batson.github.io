About Me

I'm from Melbourne, Australia, and about to finish my Bachelors Degree in Geospatial Science at RMIT University. I currently have a few gigs on the go, as a GIS and Data Analyst at the Institute for Sensible Transport, Spatial Vision, and the City of Monash. I'm passionate about using GIS to solve real world issues and improve lives, and creating effective visuals to explain complex data.

Outside of work and study you can find me nerding out over geography quizzes or fantasy basketball spreadsheets, or outside running, slamming dunks and trying to wheelie on my mountain bike.

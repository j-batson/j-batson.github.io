Calendar Atlas Project: Bushfire Affected Towns

This project was a 12 person group assignment, which aimed to create a cohesive set of maps, following the same specifications, to be compiled into a calendar - one map for each month - and distributed to local high schools. Unfortunately, due to the limitations that we experienced due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the publication of the calendar never eventuated.

Aften each member of the group pitching their idea for a theme, we settled on bushfire affected towns. After we witnessed and experienced the devastation that the Black Summer bushfires had all over Australia, our aim was to create visually appealing maps of a selection of towns that were damaged or severely affected by the bushfires, with an emphasis on encouraging tourists to return to the town.

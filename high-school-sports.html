High School Sports Map

This project was completed in 2019 as a major cartography assignment, with an open brief to create any map. I chose to explore American sport as it has such an influence on culture both domestically and internationally. The distribution and prevalence of different sports is quite interesting and certain patterns can definitely be seen. I wanted to present this information in a thematically pertinent design, and so I drew insipration straight from first hand evidence such as High School Musical, Friday Night Lights and Coach Carter, and tried to mimic the aesthetics of the varsity jackets and championship banners you can imagine hanging from a high school gym.
